KETHY BAKERS
Featured Story.Every morning at 4:00 AM, the cozy, flour-dusted kitchen of Kethy’s Local Bakery would begin to hum with activity. Kethy, a passionate baker with flour often stuck in her hair, believed in quality above all else, preparing fresh sourdough loaves and flaky pastries before the sun rose. The smell of cinnamon and warm butter drifting onto the street quickly became the local alarm clock for the entire town.
